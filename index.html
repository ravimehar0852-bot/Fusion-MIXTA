Our Story

A Fusion Born
from the Heart

Fusion Mixta started as a dream to bring bold, honest flavors to the streets of Coronel. Located on JM Erratchou 826, we've built a place where neighbors become regulars and first-timers always leave wanting more.

Our kitchen operates on a simple principle: use the freshest local ingredients, treat every dish with care, and serve every customer like family. We're proud of our 5-star reputation — and we earn it every single day.
